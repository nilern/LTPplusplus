\begin{align}
 &dh = d(u + pv) = du + d(pv) = du + pdv + vdp\\
 &\Delta h = \Delta u + p\Delta v + v\Delta p \approx v\Delta p \quad \bigg|\bigg| \quad
  \begin{aligned}
   &u(T), \Delta T = 0 \Rightarrow \Delta u = 0\\
   &\Delta\rho = 0 \Leftrightarrow \Delta v = 0
  \end{aligned}
  \\
